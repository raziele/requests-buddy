## The Ask

Or Movement requests $400,000 (₪1,250,000) in emergency funding for the Lion's Roar operation to equip and train municipal and neighborhood emergency and resilience units. The request covers physical needs (shelter and relief equipment, relief kits, community relief channel), professional needs (SAAR and intelligence equipment, training and refreshers for emergency and readiness teams), and technology and operations. Or has built resilience infrastructure with JFNA and Schusterman; municipalities are requesting assistance and the organization is currently able to provide only limited support.

**Funding Requested:** $400,000 (₪1,250,000)

**Funding Breakdown:**
- Community, relief, and shelter equipment (heaters, mattresses, bedding, lighting, chairs, blankets, burners, etc.): ₪300,000
- Relief kits and games (~500 units × ₪700): ₪350,000
- Community relief channel and webinars (stress relief, wartime coping, small business, weapons use, communicating with children): ₪50,000
- Non-consumable shelter equipment (entertainment unit, radio, water heater, TV, etc.): ₪100,000
- SAAR and intelligence equipment (generator, rescue equipment, drones, cameras): ₪150,000
- Training and refreshers for municipal emergency teams (first aid, psychological first aid, SAAR, readiness): ₪200,000
- Technology (mapping, GIS app, survey management): ₪35,000
- Operations, oversight, logistics, coordination: ₪70,000

## Context & Background

On 28 February 2026 Israel and the US launched a military campaign against Iran. The Or Communities Network activated within the first hours. A needs-assessment survey went to municipal coordinators; 23 municipalities responded. Main needs: medical and first aid equipment, search and rescue equipment, shelter outfitting, recreational/relief equipment; 21 requests for refresher courses (emergency teams, first aid, psychological first aid, SAAR reinforcement). Shelter stays average 30–45 minutes with many staying overnight; winter and shelter equipment and relief supplies are needed. On 1 March a training session was held for coordinators (emergency mapping and needs, volunteers, rescue units; community resilience and buddy mapping; relief events for children in/adjacent to shelters). Or is active in 15 local authorities (socioeconomic 4–6), 40 neighborhoods. With JFNA and Schusterman they built 4 emergency command centers, 2,500 trained volunteers, 24 authorities with trained volunteers, 18 SAAR units (Rescuer 02). During Am Kalavia, ~₪1,000,000 in emergency equipment was distributed; ~300,000 residents impacted. Many municipalities have now requested assistance; Or has provided basic relief and 5 training sessions but it is insufficient.
