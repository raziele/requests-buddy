## The Ask

NATAL is providing critical mental health support to individuals and families affected by the ongoing conflict with Iran. They have seen a significant increase in calls to their helpline and are operating in emergency mode to provide therapeutic intervention, community support, and media outreach.

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

## Context & Background

The email details NATAL's response to the "Shaagat HaAri" operation, which began on February 28, 2026. The operation has led to widespread psychological distress, anxiety, and trauma across Israel. NATAL's Helpline has experienced a 620% increase in calls related to anxiety and fear. They are also operating the "Nefesh Achat" Helpline for disabled IDF veterans and those experiencing psychological distress.

NATAL has implemented a dedicated protocol for new and ongoing patients, with therapists observing regression in some cases. They have launched an internal emergency consultation line for their therapists and are providing therapeutic intervention in hotels for displaced civilians. Community work includes webinars on coping skills and mediation, while media outreach aims to raise public awareness. The organization emphasizes the importance of resilience in the face of suffering and is directing resources towards emergency response and long-term support.
