## The Ask

The Gush Halav Local Council is urgently requesting 100 computers (20 for high school students and 80 for elementary students) to enable remote learning. Due to the ongoing conflict and proximity to the border, the community has been significantly affected, leading to school system disruptions and limited mobility. Without these computers, students will face significant educational disadvantages.

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

## Context & Background

Gush Halav, a community located approximately 4 km from the border, has suffered greatly during the recent conflict. The education system is severely disrupted, with school buildings facing shielding gaps and transportation routes to and from Gush Halav declared 'red zones.' Consequently, the return to regular schooling is expected to be significantly delayed, leaving students without alternative educational solutions. There is a critical shortage of computers in the community, which could exacerbate educational disparities and hinder the return to normalcy. The council is requesting urgent assistance to provide 20 computers for high school students and 80 for elementary school students.

**Subject: Urgent request for immediate assistance - computers for learning remotely for the children of Gush Halav**

Dear Sir/Madam,

Gush Halav, a community located about 4 km from the border, has suffered greatly during the recent period. Due to the ongoing campaign, the education system is disrupted: there are shielding gaps in the schools in particular, and the routes to and from Gush Halav have been declared "red zones." Consequently, a return to regular schooling is expected to be significantly delayed, while the children of the community have no practical alternative in the absence of a substitute framework.

The only possible solution at this time is remote learning. However, there is a severe shortage of computers in the community, which could deepen educational gaps and hinder the return to routine.

Therefore, we request your urgent assistance in supplying:

*   20 computers for high school students
*   80 computers for elementary school students

This assistance will ensure basic educational continuity and equal opportunities for the children of the community during this challenging period.

Sincerely,

Nigam Sharif
Head of the Local Council
Gush Halav Local Council
