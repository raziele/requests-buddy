## The Ask

The Haifa Municipality is requesting a donation of 50 laptops and 50 desktops with specific technical requirements to ensure the continued effective operation of municipal services during the ongoing emergency.

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

**Non-Financial Ask:** Donation of 50 laptops and 50 desktops

## Context & Background

Due to the ongoing war and its accompanying challenges, the Haifa Municipality is seeking assistance to maintain its operational capabilities. Reliable computer equipment is essential for the continuous and efficient functioning of municipal services and to support the residents of Haifa.

### Haifa Municipality Request

Date: March 05, 2026

To: Mitchashvim Association

Subject: Donation of computers to Haifa Municipality

Dear Sir/Madam,

Given the current war situation and the challenges it entails, we are turning to you with a request for assistance in equipping ourselves with computers, which will enable us to ensure the continued functioning and efficiency of the municipality, for the benefit of Haifa's residents.

Here is a list of the municipal needs:

1.  **50 Laptops**
    *   Required specifications:
        *   Intel Core i7
        *   512GB SSD Storage
        *   16GB RAM
        *   Windows 11 Pro Operating System

2.  **50 Desktops**
    *   Specifications will be provided based on existing inventory and operational needs on the ground.

3.  **Monitors**
    *   Standard size monitors for full office work, based on inventory availability.

Should a donation of computers be possible, we would appreciate an update so that we can examine technical compatibility and proceed with an orderly integration into the computer system.

Thank you in advance for considering this donation request.

Sincerely,

Dr. Galit Rand
Director of the Mayor's Office
