## The Ask

The Jewish Federations of North America, on behalf of Aharai! (Leading Change Youth), is issuing an urgent appeal to support Israel's youth during the ongoing war with Iran, referred to as the "Shaagat HaAri" War. Aharai! continues to operate despite the conflict, providing support, structure, and safe spaces for young people facing uncertainty, fear, and isolation. The organization is expanding its programming, particularly in northern Israel, and needs financial support to recruit additional instructors, replace staff serving in reserves, expand programming, and support volunteer initiatives.

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

## Context & Background

The ongoing war with Iran, "Shaagat HaAri," has severely disrupted daily life in Israel, leading to closed schools, enormous strain on families, and widespread fear and isolation among young people. Despite these challenges, Aharai! programs are actively continuing, adapting to Home Front Command guidelines by operating near protected spaces and ensuring adult staff presence. They are maintaining in-person meetings, Zoom activities, and personal mentoring to provide essential support and structure to youth.

The situation in northern Israel is particularly acute, with young people in these communities needing critical support. Aharai! is proactively expanding its programming and in-person activities, strengthening emotional resilience, organizing volunteer initiatives, reinforcing the north with additional personnel, and accelerating youth leadership programs.

The organization's staff has also been significantly impacted, with 154 members called up for reserve duty. The remaining staff are managing their own difficult personal realities while continuing to serve the youth. To sustain and expand these vital activities, Aharai! urgently needs financial support to recruit and train additional instructors, replace staff serving in the reserves, expand programming in northern communities, and ensure the safe operation of activities.

### Aharai! Urgent Appeal – Supporting Israel's Youth During the "Shaagat HaAri" War

The war with Iran, Shaagat HaAri, has once again placed Israel in an extremely challenging reality. For many communities, daily life has been completely disrupted. There is no routine. Schools and youth frameworks are closed, families are under enormous strain, and young people are facing uncertainty, fear, and isolation.

At Aharai!, we believe that precisely in moments like these, our presence is more critical than ever.

Aharai! Continues Operating Even Under Fire

Despite the ongoing war, Aharai! programs are continuing to operate in accordance with the Home Front Command guidelines, ensuring that all activities take place near protected spaces and with the required presence of two adult staff members at every activity.

Where possible, we continue to hold in person meetings, alongside Zoom activities and personal mentoring, so that the young people in our programs continue to have support, structure, and a safe space during this difficult time.

With schools closed and many frameworks paused, our teams are working tirelessly to ensure that the young people we serve are not left alone with the stress and uncertainty of the current situation.

A Special Focus on the North

The situation in northern Israel is particularly severe. Young people in these communities need us now more than ever.

Instead of waiting for government frameworks and recovery plans, Aharai! is choosing to act now:

*   Expanding programming and in-person activities when possible
*   Strengthening emotional resilience and community connection
*   Organizing volunteer initiatives and community support efforts
*   Reinforcing the north with additional Shinshinim and Mechinistim
*   Accelerating youth leadership and "launch" programs that help young people take responsibility and lead in their communities

Our Team Is Also Serving

Like many organizations across Israel, Aharai! has been deeply affected by reserve duty mobilization.

So far, 154 of our staff members, nearly one-third of the organization, have been called up to serve in the reserves, including members of our leadership team.

At the same time, many of our remaining staff are coping with extremely difficult personal realities: families with mobilized partners, communities under constant threat, and homes without adequate protection.

Despite all of this, our team continues to show up for the young people who depend on us.

To sustain our activities, we urgently need to recruit additional instructors and increase staff capacity so that we can continue to provide consistent support for our participants.

Our Youth Are Also Stepping Up

Even in the midst of war, the young people of Aharai! are demonstrating extraordinary leadership and responsibility.

Participants from Mechinat Gal Kiryat Bialik branch, for example, joined national volunteer efforts by helping pack food packages with the organization Leket.

Others visited public shelters across the city, bringing activities, crafts, and moments of joy for children and families spending long hours in protected spaces.

These small acts of kindness create something much larger: community resilience.

Our participants refuse to stand on the sidelines. They choose responsibility, solidarity, and action.

We Need Your Help Now

In times of war, the Israeli economy slows dramatically and philanthropic resources become even more critical.

At the same time, the need for youth support has never been greater.

Aharai! is not scaling back, we are expanding our activities to meet the moment.

But to do so, we urgently need financial support to:

*   Recruit and train additional instructors
*   Replace staff members serving in the reserves
*   Expand programming in northern communities
*   Operate safe in person activities near protected spaces
*   Run virtual programming and personal mentoring
*   Support volunteer initiatives and resilience activities for youth

Stand With Israel's Next Generation

The young people of Israel are facing one of the most difficult periods in recent years. They need stability, leadership, and hope.

At Aharai!, we are committed to being there for them today, not a year from now.

Your support will allow us to continue standing with Israel's youth and strengthening the communities that depend on them.

We urgently ask for your partnership and financial support at this critical time.

Donations can be made to Bank Leumi, Branch 805, Account No. 1896246, name of the association “Noar Movil Shinui” (Leading Change Youth).

For any questions or additional information, please contact us at: contact@aharai.org.il

Together, we can ensure that even in the midst of war, Israel's next generation continues to grow stronger, more resilient, and ready to lead.

Thank you for standing with Aharai! and with the young people of Israel.
