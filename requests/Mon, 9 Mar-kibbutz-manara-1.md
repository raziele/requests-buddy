## Context & Background

The Kibbutz Manara is facing an emergency situation and requires immediate assistance to equip its 30-person emergency response team. The team has identified critical needs for tactical uniforms, Lowa shoes, radio headsets, softshells, and markers. The shoes are particularly urgent, as some team members are using damaged footwear.

The total estimated cost for this essential equipment is 70,000 NIS. The request is being relayed by Iris Hen, the Resource Development Manager for Kibbutz Manara, who is seeking assistance from Barak Loozon of the Jewish Federation.
