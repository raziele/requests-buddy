## The Ask

Tikkun and the Hamahanot HaOlim Youth Movement are mobilizing to support Israeli communities facing ongoing attacks by providing educational and community programs. They are seeking donations to fund volunteer housing, transportation, staff coordination, training, equipment, and food. The estimated cost for the coming week is $25,000.

**Funding Requested:** $25,000

**Funding Breakdown:**
- Volunteer housing, transportation, insurance, staff coordination, training, equipment, food: $25,000

## Context & Background

On February 28, 2026, Operation “Roaring Lion” began, with hundreds of missiles and drones launched from Iran toward Israeli cities. This has led to ongoing attacks, forcing Israelis to move into bomb shelters and experience constant fear and uncertainty. Tikkun and the Hamahanot HaOlim Youth Movement have mobilized to support communities and strengthen the civilian front through educational and community programs.

They aim to provide stability and support for children and youth during this difficult time. Their efforts include educational programs in municipal shelters, educational centers for evacuated families, programs for teens in the Eshkol region near Gaza, and community activities tailored to local needs and municipalities.

The estimated cost for the coming week's activities is $25,000, which will fund volunteer housing, transportation, insurance, staff coordination, training, equipment, and food.
