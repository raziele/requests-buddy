## The Ask

Mechinat Shuvu Achim, a nationwide humanitarian project operating in memory of the three abductees and in cooperation with welfare departments from Kiryat Shmona to Sderot, is requesting monetary or in-kind support to meet urgent needs arising during Operation "Roar of the Lion." In the past week and a half they have received many requests from the field: children in shelters who cannot afford computers for remote learning, soldiers who have been in the field since the start of the operation and need emergency kits, and elderly who need hygiene products, medicine, and basic food. They are asking the Chicago Federation to consider a donation or in-kind contribution for these populations. The association holds all required documentation for tax purposes.

## Context & Background

Mechinat Shuvu Achim runs a nationwide humanitarian project that assists vulnerable populations in cooperation with welfare departments from Kiryat Shmona to Sderot. The project operates in memory of the three abductees.

During Operation "Roar of the Lion" (Shagat HaAryeh), over the past week and a half, the organization has been receiving many requests from the field. Affected populations include: families of reservists who have been economically impacted by the call-up; evacuated families; lone soldiers; and elderly. Concrete needs have crystallized into three categories: (1) children staying in shelters who are required to learn remotely but whose families cannot afford computers; (2) soldiers who were mobilized at the start of the operation and have been in the field for a week and a half and need emergency kits; and (3) elderly who need hygiene products, medicine, and basic food.

The organization is reaching out to explore the possibility of monetary support or in-kind donations (products) for these populations in light of the current need and timing. They state that the association holds all documents required for tax purposes and express interest in a partnership with the federation.
