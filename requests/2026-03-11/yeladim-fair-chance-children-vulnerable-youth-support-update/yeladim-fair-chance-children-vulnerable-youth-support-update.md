## The Ask

Yeladim - Fair Chance for Children shares a short update from the past week describing how the current security situation is affecting the vulnerable children and young adults they serve. The message relates several anonymized vignettes: a single mother with three young girls who moved into a school shelter and had almost no food until Yeladim brought supplies; a reservist whose small business and income have dropped when he is called up; a young woman with chronic illness who has almost stopped eating; a young woman choosing between medication and food; and a 16-year-old in their legal guardianship whose refrigerator was empty until they provided support. The organization emphasizes that they stay in close contact, check in, and step in where help is most urgent, and that steady, reliable support is more important than ever.
