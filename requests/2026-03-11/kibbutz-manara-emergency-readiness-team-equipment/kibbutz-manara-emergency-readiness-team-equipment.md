## Context & Background

Kibbutz Manara, in Northern Israel, has submitted a request for emergency support to equip its community readiness/response team (כיתת כוננות). The request was sent by Iris Hen (Resource Development Manager) to Barak Loozon at the Jewish Federation Israel Office, following prior correspondence.
