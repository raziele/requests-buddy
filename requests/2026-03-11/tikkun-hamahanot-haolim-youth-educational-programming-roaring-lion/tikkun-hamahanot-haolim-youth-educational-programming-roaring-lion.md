## The Ask

Tikkun and the Hamahanot HaOlim Youth Movement are requesting support to provide stability and educational programming for children and youth during Operation "Roaring Lion." Goals include strengthening community resilience, reducing stress and anxiety, creating safe spaces, and supporting local communities. Activities include educational centers for children from evacuated families, educational programs in municipal shelters, programs for teens in the Eshkol region, and community activities tailored to local needs. Estimated cost for the coming week: $25,000.

**Funding Requested:** $25,000

## Context & Background

Operation "Roaring Lion" began February 28, 2026, with hundreds of missiles and drones launched from Iran toward Israeli cities. Tikkun and Hamahanot HaOlim mobilized to support communities and strengthen the civilian front. Donations fund volunteer housing, transportation, insurance, staff coordination, training, equipment, and food. The initiative focuses on educational programs in shelters, centers for evacuated families' children, programs for teens near Gaza, and tailored community activities.
