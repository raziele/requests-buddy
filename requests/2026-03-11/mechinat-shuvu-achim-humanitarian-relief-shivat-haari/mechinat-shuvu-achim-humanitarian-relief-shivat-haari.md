## The Ask

Mechinat Shuvu Achim, a nationwide humanitarian mechina operating in memory of the three abductees, is requesting donations or in-kind product support for populations affected by Operation Shivat HaAri (Lion's Roar). During the past week and a half they have received many requests from the field: children in shelters who cannot afford computers for remote learning, soldiers deployed since the start of the operation who need emergency kits, and elderly who need hygiene products, medicine, and basic food. The organization holds all required documentation for tax purposes and is asking the Chicago Federation to consider financial or product contributions.

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

## Context & Background

Mechinat Shuvu Achim runs a nationwide humanitarian project in cooperation with welfare departments from Kiryat Shmona to Sderot, supporting vulnerable populations. The mechina is established in memory of the three abductees.

Since the start of Operation Shivat HaAri, the organization has been receiving a high volume of requests from the field. Affected groups include: reservist families suffering economic hardship due to call-up; evacuated families; lone soldiers; and elderly. Concrete needs have crystallized into three categories: (1) children in shelters who must learn remotely but whose families cannot afford computers; (2) soldiers who were mobilized at the outset of the operation and have been in the field for over a week, requiring emergency kits; and (3) elderly needing hygiene products, medicine, and basic food.

The organization is reaching out to explore the possibility of donations or in-kind product contributions to address these needs. They have indicated that the association holds all documents required for tax purposes. The request is framed as a partnership opportunity in light of current need and timing.
