## The Ask

Hamal Ezrachi is launching Operation Calm in the Shelter to provide emotional relief kits for children spending extended hours in shelters during ongoing security alerts. Kits include emotion cards, stress-reduction games, and guided calming activities for grades 1–6. The initiative will reach 14,000 children across 10 municipalities and may provide limited shelter support (e.g. mattresses/mats) when requested by local authorities. Total budget: $250,000.

**Funding Requested:** $250,000

## Context & Background

Thousands of children across northern and central Israel are spending extended hours in shelters; many experience increasing anxiety and emotional distress while parents are exhausted. In response to urgent requests from local authorities, BSI is deploying emotional relief kits developed by their pedagogical team, designed for shelter environments. The initiative can be deployed within days using existing infrastructure and municipal partnerships. Expected impact: emotional support for 14,000 children, relief for families, distribution across 10 municipalities.
