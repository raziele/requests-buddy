## The Ask

Mashiv HaRuach is seeking support to renovate and establish an additional workshop space at the Metsoke Dragot retreat site. This expansion aims to double their capacity to provide trauma-informed resilience workshops for frontline caregivers, addressing the growing demand and reducing waiting times. The project requires $150,000 to complete.

**Funding Requested:** $150,000

**Funding Breakdown:**
- The Full Healing Center: $150,000
- Main Workshop Hall: $75,000
- Private Meeting Room: $50,000
- Outdoor Healing Garden: $25,000

**Non-Financial Ask:** Partners to make this vision real.

## Context & Background

Mashiv HaRuach, established in late 2023, provides trauma-informed resilience workshops for Israel's frontline caregivers. They have served over 2,000 participants and have a growing demand that is currently limited by infrastructure. The organization is partnering with Metsoke Dragot to renovate an additional building, creating a new workshop space that will double their capacity.

The project includes a central workshop hall, a private meeting room, and a landscaped outdoor healing garden. The total funding needed is $150,000, with a timeline of 3 months from funding to completion. Naming opportunities are available for the renovated spaces. The organization emphasizes the critical need for this expansion to support those who are supporting others during this challenging time.
