## The Ask

The Elah Center is proposing a program to provide support and supervision for social workers in municipal social service departments who are experiencing heightened stress and vicarious trauma due to the ongoing war. The program will offer ongoing group and individual supervision, short-term workshops, and educational lectures, and they are seeking support to implement this much-needed response.

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

**Non-Financial Ask:** Generous support to continue working to strengthen social resilience by supporting social workers.

## Context & Background

The Elah Center, which specializes in treating trauma, bereavement, and loss, is proposing a nationwide program to support social workers in municipal social service departments. These professionals are facing unprecedented emotional and social burdens due to the prolonged warfare since October 2023, and now with the escalation with Iran. They are at significant risk of vicarious trauma, secondary traumatization, and compassion fatigue.

The proposed program includes ongoing group supervision sessions, individual supervision and personal support for staff, short-term workshops, and educational lectures on topics such as trauma and self-care. The Elah Center seeks to collaborate with local authorities and governmental bodies to provide this comprehensive support, which is essential for maintaining the resilience of Israeli society and ensuring the continuity of services. The CEO is personally reaching out to request support for this initiative.
