## The Ask

Tikkun and Hamahanot HaOlim Youth Movement are requesting support for their emergency educational response during Operation Roaring Lion (begun February 28, 2026). Programs include educational centers for children from evacuated families, educational programs for children and teens in municipal shelters, programs for teens in the Eshkol region near Gaza, and community activities tailored to local needs. Estimated cost for the coming week: $25,000.

**Funding Requested:** $25,000

## Context & Background

Operation Roaring Lion began February 28, 2026, with hundreds of missiles and drones launched from Iran toward Israeli cities. Tikkun and Hamahanot HaOlim mobilized to support communities and strengthen the civilian front through educational and community programs. Goals include strengthening community resilience, reducing stress and anxiety, creating safe spaces for processing and relief, and supporting and activating local communities. Donations fund volunteer housing, transportation, insurance, staff coordination, training, equipment, food, and related costs.
