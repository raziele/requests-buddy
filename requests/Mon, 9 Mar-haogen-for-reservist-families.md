## The Ask

HaOgen for Reservist Families is requesting funding to support reservist families facing increased demand due to a massive mobilization. They are providing immediate relief (meals, essential supplies) for families whose homes were hit by missiles, expanding meal provisions through grocery vouchers, running daily Zoom workshops for children, and providing activity kits for children in bomb shelters.

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

**Funding Breakdown:**
- Immediate relief (meals, essential supplies) for families whose homes were hit by missiles: $650 per family
- Grocery vouchers for reservist families: 10,000 vouchers
- Daily Zoom workshops for children of reservists: $900 per session
- Activity kits for children in bomb shelters: $12 per family

## Context & Background

Due to a large-scale military mobilization in Israel, HaOgen for Reservist Families is experiencing unprecedented demand for their services. The organization provides critical support to families of reservists, helping them cope with the overwhelming difficulties of the current situation.

Specific challenges include mothers managing children alone during sirens, parents needing assistance with pet care while they cannot leave their children, and essential workers requiring childcare due to school closures. HaOgen has responded by increasing volunteer support and expanding their reach to new cities, matching volunteers closely to families in need.

The organization is seeking funding for several new programs to address these urgent needs. These include immediate relief for families whose homes have been destroyed by missiles, increased meal provisions through grocery vouchers, educational and engaging Zoom workshops for children, and activity kits to occupy children during extended periods in bomb shelters.
