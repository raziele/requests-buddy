## The Ask

ELEM is requesting support to continue and expand its services for at-risk youth during Operation "Roaring Lion." They are extending digital outreach support hours and reinforcing staffing at their 24/7 shelters in Jerusalem, Haifa, and Tel Aviv to provide immediate, trauma-informed care. This support is crucial for maintaining operational continuity and responding swiftly to emerging needs.

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

## Context & Background

ELEM is providing essential support to teens and young people during a period of conflict, Operation "Roaring Lion." Despite rapidly shifting circumstances and safety guidelines necessitating remote service delivery, ELEM has maintained continuous contact and provided tailored support. They have proactively expanded their digital outreach services to operate until midnight on weekdays with reinforced, specially trained teams. Additionally, their 24/7 shelters in Jerusalem, Haifa, and Tel Aviv have received strengthened professional staffing to ensure immediate, trauma-informed care for youth at the highest risk.
