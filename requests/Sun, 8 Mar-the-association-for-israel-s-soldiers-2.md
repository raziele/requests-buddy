## The Ask

The Association for Israel's Soldiers is conducting 'Operation Roaring Lion' to provide critical support to IDF soldiers and their families during the ongoing conflict. They are seeking donations for essential personal necessities, life-saving medical equipment, support for impacted families, and financial aid for wounded soldiers and bereaved families. 100% of donations go directly to benefit soldiers.

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

**Funding Breakdown:**
- Male hygiene kit: 200 NIS
- Female hygiene kit: 150 NIS
- Physical & Mental Recharge (per soldier): approx. 100 NIS
- Physical & Mental Recharge (per military unit): 40-250k NIS
- Financial aid for evacuated families: 2000 NIS
- Financial aid for moderately or seriously injured soldiers' families: 5,000 NIS
- Financial aid for bereaved families in mourning: 2,000 NIS
- IDF Operational Ambulance: 650k NIS

## Context & Background

In light of the security developments since February 28th, 2026, the Association for Israel's Soldiers is taking proactive and decisive steps to support IDF soldiers. Through the Association's Emergency Relief Fund, they aim to procure essential supplies without delay. These supplies include basic personal necessities (hygiene, clothing, non-tactical equipment), life-saving medical equipment (military ambulances, mobile medical units), and support for impacted families (assistance for evacuated families, bereaved families, and wounded soldiers). The "Operation Roaring Lion" campaign is a direct response to the ongoing conflict, emphasizing that 100% of all donations go directly to benefiting soldiers with no overhead costs. The Association for Israel's Soldiers is authorized by the IDF to receive and distribute donations for the welfare of IDF soldiers. They have a proven track record of transferring significant donations to the IDF, benefiting soldier welfare. The IDF appreciates and praises the organization's activities and contribution.

**ISRAEL DEFENSE FORCES (IDF) OUR MISSION**

The IDF, the State of Israel's military, was founded to defend the country, shield its residents, protect its independence, and foil attempts by its enemies to disrupt routine life. Military personnel are sworn to comply with the IDF's commands and values. The Association for Israel's Soldiers nonprofit is the sole organization authorized by the IDF to receive and distribute donations for the welfare and well-being of IDF soldiers. 100% of all donations go directly to benefit the servicepeople.

**Operation "Roaring Lion” – Defending Israel**

In light of the security developments since February 28th 2026, the Association for Israel's Soldiers is taking proactive and decisive steps to support our soldiers. Through the Association's Emergency Relief Fund, we enable the IDF and Israel's Ministry of Defense to procure essential supplies for soldiers without delay, including:

1.  **Basic personal necessities** – hygiene supplies, non-military clothing, personal non-tactical equipment, and resources that provide physical and emotional respite.
2.  **Life-saving medical equipment** – military ambulances, mobile medical units, and other critical life-preserving medical gear.
3.  **Support for impacted families** – assistance for families evacuated from their homes, wounded soldiers and their families, bereaved families in mourning, and individuals from vulnerable populations, including financial vouchers for essential needs and emergency assistance grants.

100% of all donations go directly to benefiting soldiers — with no overhead costs and no administrative delays.

**THE IDF DONATION POLICY**

During times of crisis, Israel and its supporters worldwide strengthen their resolve to support and embrace the people of Israel, the IDF and its dedicated soldiers. The Israeli military provides its soldiers with all the gear and items they need for war, resupplying based on IDF-defined needs and the gaps that arise. The IDF is the sole organization responsible for procuring personal protective equipment for soldiers and gear required for military operations. In light of the Swords of Iron War, army policies allow the IDF to receive donations from an emergency fund that can deliver immediate solutions to the IDF's needs and benefit its soldiers.
