## The Ask

Google AI Studio is notifying you of upcoming Gemini API billing policy changes: new optional project-level spend caps starting March 16, 2026 and enforced billing account tier caps starting April 1, 2026. You are asked to review your current usage and billing account spend to avoid service disruption when the new caps take effect, and to request a higher cap override if needed.

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

**Non-Financial Ask:** Manually verify current Usage Tier and applicable limits, review historical monthly spend for your billing account, and request a billing account cap override via a provided form if your automatically assigned tier cap is too low.

## Context & Background

Google AI Studio (Gemini API) is introducing changes to Usage Tiers and billing caps that may affect ongoing API usage.

Starting March 16, 2026, you can configure optional project-level spend caps; if a project exceeds its configured target, requests for that project will be paused for approximately 10 minutes, after which projects resume when the monthly limits reset (or when you update/remove the cap).

Starting April 1, 2026, each Usage Tier will enforce an enforced maximum monthly spend limit at the billing account level. If aggregate spend reaches the cap, Gemini API requests tied to that billing account will be paused until the next month. Tier upgrades will be automated as accounts meet specified spend and tenure thresholds.

To prepare, the email asks you to review your current Usage Tier and limits, evaluate your historical monthly spend to determine whether the new billing account cap will impact your projects, and submit an override request if you need a higher cap than the tier automatically assigns.
