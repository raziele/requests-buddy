## The Ask

Yesh Matsav requests $50,000 in emergency funding to scale its youth resilience response in Northern Israel. Since late February 2026, Northern Israel has been under sustained attack; the organization aims to deploy immediately to 400 youth and 100 educators via teacher training (Resilience Hubs), life-changing role-model encounters, shelter crisis kits for 20 schools, and an AI-powered mentorship tool. Yesh Matsav has a proven track record (2,000 youth in 2025, 80% showing significant improvement) and existing partnerships with the President of Israel and the Ministry of Education.

**Funding Requested:** $50,000

**Funding Breakdown:**
- Teacher Training: Establishing Resilience Hubs for 100 educators in Northern schools: $20,000
- Life-Changing Encounters: Delivering 10+ Key Experience sessions with inspiring role models to 300+ youth in bomb shelters: $15,000
- Shelter Crisis Kits: Yesh Matsav resilience kits for 20 schools: $10,000
- Digital Resilience (AI Mentor): Scaling AI-powered mentorship for 24/7 real-time guidance and support: $5,000

**Non-Financial Ask:** Distribution and visibility—contact thanked Barak for the offer to spread and help; original ask included alignment on emergency relief fund availability and a brief call to align plan and budget.

## Context & Background

Yesh Matsav is an Israeli non-profit supporting at-risk youth impacted by the current war, with leadership including Dr. Lior Zoref (former Microsoft VP, TED speaker), Yaniv Rivlin (Bird Israel), and education and tech figures. They deliver teacher/school support and inspiring role models; in 2025 they reached 2,000 youth, with 80% showing significant improvement in motivation and achievements. Since October 7th they have worked in the Gaza envelope (Nofey Habsor), ORT Shlomi in the North, with IDF orphans and widows, and in Bat Yam during the first Iran war in 2025.

Northern Israel has been under relentless assault since Hezbollah joined the war on February 28, 2026. In the week prior to the request, over 210 rockets and drones targeted the Galilee and Haifa, with thousands of youth spending days in bomb shelters. Yesh Matsav positions itself as a "Resilience Anchor" for schools in conflict zones, turning trauma into growth by training teachers as personal coaches for high-risk youth.

The $50,000 emergency request would allow immediate deployment to 400 additional youth and 100 educators in the North through Resilience Hubs, Key Experience sessions in shelters, crisis kits for 20 schools, and scaling of their AI mentorship tool. The CEO reported that since Saturday his phone had not stopped with messages about previously stable kids suddenly at risk, and the organization is responding by equipping educators across temporary housing, shelters, and classrooms.

Yesh Matsav (yeshmatsav.org/en) is a non-profit organization based in Israel, dedicated to supporting at-risk youth impacted by the current war in Israel, helping to rebuild Israel's future.

Board and founders include Dr. Lior Zoref (former Microsoft VP, TED speaker, best-selling author), Yaniv Rivlin (serial entrepreneur, founding CEO of Bird Israel), Hana David (leading figure in Israeli education), and Yosi Taguri (serial entrepreneur, CEO of ZipRecruiter Israel).

**Programs:**
