## The Ask

Request for program funding to support a project strengthening resilience and parental authority among wives of IDF reservists during Israel's ongoing conflict.

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

**Non-Financial Ask:** Support for a comprehensive project to strengthen resilience and parental authority among wives of IDF reservists

## Context & Background

During Israel's longest war, these women became de facto single mothers while their partners served in reserve forces. The Nitzan Association seeks funding to provide a broad support system for these women who bear significant burdens.
