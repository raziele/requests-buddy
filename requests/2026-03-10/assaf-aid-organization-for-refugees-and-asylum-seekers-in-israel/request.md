## The Ask

ASSAF is requesting urgent funding to expand remote psychosocial support services and emergency telephone lines for refugee communities during Israel's ongoing security crisis, which has closed educational facilities and workplaces while refugees remain excluded from national emergency assistance.

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

**Non-Financial Ask:** Expand remote psychosocial support services and emergency telephone lines for refugee communities

## Context & Background

Israel is experiencing continuous missile attacks and emergency regulations that have closed schools and workplaces. Refugee communities, already excluded from national safety nets, face heightened vulnerability without access to emergency assistance or social protection. ASSAF has adapted services to operate in emergency mode, mapping urgent needs and strengthening resilience support while maintaining multilingual information dissemination and expanded reception hours.
