## The Ask

HaOgen for Reservist Families is seeking funding for four emergency programs supporting Israeli reservist families during the current mobilization. Programs include: 1) immediate relief for families whose homes were hit by missiles ($650/family), 2) increased meal provision for single parents ($900/session), 3) daily Zoom workshops for children ($900/session), and 4) activity kits for children in bomb shelters ($12/family).

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

**Non-Financial Ask:** Funding for specific emergency programs supporting reservist families during Israel's current mobilization.

## Context & Background

HaOgen for Reservist Families is experiencing increased demand due to Israel's ongoing mobilization. They provide critical support to reservist families facing missile attacks, single parents managing alone, and children in bomb shelters. Current programs include meal provision, Zoom workshops, and activity kits. Funding is needed to sustain these vital services during the emergency.
