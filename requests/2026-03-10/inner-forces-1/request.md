## The Ask

Inner Forces seeks funding to expand immediate resilience support groups for mothers of combat soldiers and wives of reservists during the ongoing emergency. The organization provides emotional support and practical coping tools through professionally facilitated online groups.

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

**Non-Financial Ask:** Share information with federation network colleagues and potential donors in your community.

## Context & Background

Inner Forces supports families of IDF soldiers and security forces members, who face significant emotional and economic burdens during prolonged security crises. With 30% of Israel's population being immediate family members of service members, the organization operates two flagship programs: Community Resilience Ambassador Program and Occupational Resilience Program. Current emergency response includes launching immediate resilience support groups for mothers of combat soldiers and wives of reservists.
