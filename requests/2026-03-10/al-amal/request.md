## The Ask

Al Amal seeks an introduction to connect with the Jewish Federation for potential funding support for their environmental advocacy programs targeting Arab and Jewish communities in the Galilee. The NGO focuses on joint environmental initiatives, shared society work, and climate action.

**Funding Requested:** $107,000

**Funding Breakdown:**
- Galilee Mayors Forum annual cost: $52,000
- Youth groups program annual cost: $55,000

**Non-Financial Ask:** Introduction to the Jewish Federation for potential funding partnership

## Context & Background

Al Amal is an environmental NGO working in Israel's Arab minority communities and shared Jewish-Arab dynamics. They focus on joint environmental initiatives, climate action, and shared society development. The organization has established programs including the Galilee Mayors Forum and youth groups, with specific funding needs for these initiatives.
