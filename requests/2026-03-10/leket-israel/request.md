## The Ask

Emergency funding request of $508,210 per week to maintain food distribution operations during Israel's war with Iran. Current supply disruptions have reduced meal distribution from 8,000 to 3,000 daily meals, requiring $5,000 daily food purchases and supermarket vouchers for affected families.

**Funding Requested:** $508,210 per week

**Funding Breakdown:**
- Meals: $5,000 daily
- Produce: Temporary purchases to maintain supply

**Non-Financial Ask:** Temporary supermarket vouchers for families unable to access distribution points

## Context & Background

Due to rocket fire and operational disruptions, Leket Israel's food rescue operations have been severely impacted. Volunteer activity has been paused, harvest collections halted, and IDF bases (primary suppliers) are inaccessible. This has reduced surplus food availability by 62%, threatening food security for vulnerable families during the ongoing conflict.
