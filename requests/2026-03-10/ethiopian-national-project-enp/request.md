## The Ask

Ethiopian National Project (ENP) requests ongoing support to continue their SPACE program, which provides educational stability and empowerment for Ethiopian-Israeli children facing disruption and challenges. Their work integrates Ethiopian-Israeli youth into Israeli society while contributing to national security and development.

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

**Non-Financial Ask:** Solidarity and continued support from federations and foundations to broaden impact and empower the community.

## Context & Background

During challenging times, ENP's SPACE program serves as a vital anchor for Ethiopian-Israeli children, helping them navigate complex realities with hope and confidence. Their work has led to increased integration of Ethiopian-Israelis into society and national institutions, but sustained funding is needed to maintain and expand these critical services.
