## The Ask

Israel Women's Network requests urgent funding to expand their Alice Line emergency hotline capacity to support women affected by Israel's war with Iran, addressing immediate needs for legal assistance, policy advocacy, and crisis intervention.

**Funding Requested:** $500,000

**Non-Financial Ask:** Expand hotline capacity, increase legal staffing, extend multilingual outreach, and strengthen real-time policy advocacy

## Context & Background

Women in Israel face severe impacts from the ongoing war with Iran, including economic instability, childcare burdens, and lack of legal protections. The Israel Women's Network's Alice Line has seen a surge in emergency calls and requires immediate funding to scale operations and provide critical support.
