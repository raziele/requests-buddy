## The Ask

The equipment is essential for responding to missile strikes and building collapses across the country.

ZAKA National Emergency Rescue Equipment Initiative

Equipping Israel's Civilian Search & Rescue Volunteers

Date: March 5, 2026

Ofer Lichtig - Israel Representative
Greater Miami Jewish Federation

Dear Ofer,

In light of the escalating security situation in Israel and the continued attacks on civilian communities, ZAKA volunteers are operating at the front lines of complex emergency scenes across the country, including missile impacts from Iran and its regional proxies, structural collapses, and mass-casualty incidents.

Recently, ZAKA teams responded to a direct missile strike on a residential building in Beit Shemesh in which nine civilians were killed. Volunteers arrived within minutes and worked alongside Israel's security and rescue forces under extremely difficult conditions of structural destruction, darkness, and ongoing danger.

ZAKA's national volunteer network includes more than 3,850 volunteers, including approximately 900 specially trained by Israel's Home Front Command in advanced search-and-rescue operations. These teams frequently operate inside unstable structures following missile strikes and building collapses.

To ensure these volunteers can continue responding effectively, ZAKA is mobilizing resources to procure essential technical search-and-rescue equipment for its Home Front Command-trained civilian rescue unit - the only volunteer unit in Israel authorized to participate in engineering-level rescue operations at collapsed structures.

Priority Search & Rescue Equipment Needs

Equipment | Purpose | Unit Cost | Quantity | Total
--- | --- | --- | --- | ---
Thermal Imaging Systems | Locating trapped victims in rubble and darkness | $5,200 | 12 | $62,400
Portable Lighting Towers | Enable rescue operations during nighttime missile strikes and blackouts | $18,885 | 60 | $1,133,100
Firefighter Rescue Cutting Tools | Breaching metal and reinforced structures to free trapped victims | $12,300 | 18 | $221,400
Ambulance Rescue Tool Kits | Basic rescue tools for initial response at incident scenes | $2,600 | 60 | $156,000

Total Immediate Emergency Equipment Needs: $1,572,900

Given the increasing number of simultaneous emergency incidents occurring across the country, ZAKA must equip dozens of response teams to ensure rapid deployment and effective response nationwide.

Your partnership in supporting this essential rescue equipment would significantly strengthen Israel's civilian emergency resilience and ensure that ZAKA volunteers can continue responding professionally and safely at disaster sites, both rescuing those in distress, while upholding the organization's sacred mission of preserving human dignity even under the most difficult circumstances.

We would welcome the opportunity to partner with you to equip ZAKA's volunteer rescue teams responding to missile strikes and structural collapse incidents across Israel.

With sincere appreciation,

Duby Weissenstern
Chief Executive Officer
