## The Ask

ELEM seeks support to maintain operations and expand services for teens and young people affected by the ongoing conflict in Israel. They need funding to sustain remote support programs, strengthen 24/7 shelters, and ensure trauma-informed care for those at highest risk.

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

**Non-Financial Ask:** Support to respond swiftly, expand services, and maintain operational continuity under challenging conditions.

## Context & Background

Since the launch of Operation "Roaring Lion," ELEM has maintained continuous remote support for teens and young people through digital outreach and strengthened 24/7 shelters in Jerusalem, Haifa, and Tel Aviv. They require funding to sustain these services and expand as needed during the ongoing conflict.
