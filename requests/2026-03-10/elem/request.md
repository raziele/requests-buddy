### Emergency Activities _ March 2026.pdf

March 8, 2026

ELEM Emergency Response

Current Needs

In light of the ongoing war, ELEM has shifted to emergency operations to ensure continuity of care for vulnerable teens and young adults.

Emergency Programs Requiring Additional Funding

1. Reinforcement of ELEM Digital (National Service)
ELEM Digital is a nationwide online support service available to all teens in Israel, not only those previously engaged with ELEM.
Due to the current situation, we are reinforcing staffing to expand support, both in Hebrew and Arabic, for teens who are unable to leave their homes and are experiencing heightened emotional distress. The increase in demand is already evident among both Hebrew and Arabic speaking youth.

2. Strengthening 24/7 Shelters – Tel Aviv, Jerusalem, Haifa
Our shelters operate around the clock and are staffed by ELEM’s professional teams.
We are increasing staffing levels to ensure continuous, high-quality, trauma-informed care. The young people in our shelters are experiencing intensified triggers and emotional distress and require significantly more support than usual, particularly as they must remain indoors around the clock. In addition to current residents, graduates who had transitioned to independent living are now returning during the day because they need support and do not want to remain alone.

3. Calming Kits for Shelter Residents
We are preparing calming kits for youth staying in our shelters. These kits include practical items designed to help reduce anxiety and regulate stress, such as headphones, coloring booklets, stress balls, and other grounding tools. The kits are designated for the young people in our direct care.

4. Food Baskets
We have identified a growing need for food assistance in certain areas among the population we serve. In Eilat, our team has already distributed food baskets, and we are working to respond to additional needs as they arise.
