## The Ask

ZAKA requests emergency rescue equipment (thermal imaging systems, portable lighting towers, firefighter tools) to support volunteer teams responding to missile strikes and structural collapses. Partnership with Miami Federation would strengthen Israel's civilian emergency resilience.

**Funding Requested:** $1,572,900

**Funding Breakdown:**
- Thermal Imaging Systems: $5,200
- Portable Lighting Towers: $18,885
- Firefighter Rescue Cutting Tools: $12,300
- Ambulance Rescue Tool Kits: $2,600

## Context & Background

ZAKA volunteers operate at front lines of missile strikes and structural collapses across Israel. Requires equipment to enable safe operations in unstable structures. Current equipment shortages hinder response capabilities.
