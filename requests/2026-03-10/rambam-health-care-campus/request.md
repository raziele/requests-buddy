## The Ask

Rambam Health Care Campus requests $8 million in funding to complete construction and purchase equipment for a fortified ICU to treat critically ill patients during ongoing conflicts.

**Funding Requested:** $8,000,000

**Funding Breakdown:**
- Construction: $5,000,000
- Equipment: $3,000,000

## Context & Background

Rambam's Fortified Underground Emergency Hospital is treating 700 patients during missile attacks. The existing unfortified ICU cannot safely relocate patients during sirens. A new fortified ICU is urgently needed to provide uninterrupted care, with $13.5M already raised and $8M still required.
