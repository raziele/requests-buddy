## The Ask

Dror Israel seeks $360,000 in emergency funding to continue providing educational, emotional, and trauma support to 200,000 participants nationwide during the ongoing conflict with Iran. The funds will support childcare for essential workers, educational activities in evacuee hotels, public shelter operations, pedagogical support for schools, and resilience programs through dance.

**Funding Requested:** $360,000

**Funding Breakdown:**
- Emergency Childcare for Essential Workers: $125,000
- Educational activities at evacuee hotels: $10,000
- Organizing activities in public shelters: $60,000
- Pedagogy of Hope: $80,000
- Resilience through Community Movement and Dance: $10,000

## Context & Background

Israel is experiencing ongoing missile attacks from Iran, forcing civilians into bomb shelters and displacing families. Dror Israel's educators provide trauma support and educational programming in shelters, evacuee centers, and homes. The organization needs immediate funding to maintain operations, support 200,000 participants, and respond to emerging needs.
