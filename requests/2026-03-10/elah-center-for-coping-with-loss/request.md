## The Ask

Elah Center seeks $350,000 in emergency funding for immediate support programs including trauma therapy groups, individual counseling, and social support services for war-affected populations, elderly individuals, and Arab community members.

**Funding Requested:** $350,000

**Funding Breakdown:**
- Emergency Childcare for Essential Workers: $125,000
- Educational activities at evacuee hotels: $10,000
- Organizing activities in public shelters: $60,000
- Pedagogy of Hope: $80,000
- Resilience through Community Movement and Dance: $10,000

**Non-Financial Ask:** Partnership in raising resources for ongoing support programs

## Context & Background

Following the Iron Swords War and ongoing conflict with Iran, Elah Center seeks emergency funding to continue trauma therapy services for war-affected populations, elderly individuals, and Arab community members. Programs include support groups, individual counseling, and social support services. Funding is needed immediately to maintain operations and provide critical mental health support during this crisis.
