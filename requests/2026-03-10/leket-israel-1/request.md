## The Ask

Emergency funding request of $508,210 weekly to maintain food distribution operations during the Israel-Iran war, which has disrupted Leket Israel's food rescue network and reduced daily meals from 8,000 to 3,000.

**Funding Requested:** $508,210

**Non-Financial Ask:** Maintain operations and ensure food continues reaching vulnerable families during the crisis

## Context & Background

Due to the Israel-Iran war, Leket Israel's food rescue operations have been severely disrupted by rocket fire, closed corporate offices, and IDF base restrictions. This has reduced their daily meal distribution from 8,000 to 3,000. They need $508,210 weekly to purchase additional meals and produce to maintain operations and prevent further hardship for vulnerable families.
