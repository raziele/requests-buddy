## The Ask

Kadima Youth Homes network is requesting support to continue operating without interruption during the Roar of the Lion campaign. From the first day they confirmed the safety of all staff and youth and have been running a detailed schedule of daily Zoom group activities, remote learning support, and individual WhatsApp/Video/Zoom contact so every child feels seen and heard. Staff received training on emotional processes in stress and anxiety and practical tools for calming and age-appropriate conversation. They are building resilience through mutual care for youth and staff and ask for donations and volunteer engagement to maintain a stable, safe home for youth during uncertainty.

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

**Non-Financial Ask:** Donations; volunteering (contact 052-6879609)

## Context & Background

Since the opening of Operation Roar of the Lion, the Kadima Youth Homes network has been operating continuously and without pause. On the first Saturday they verified with all guidance staff and youth that everyone was safe and well, and from Sunday morning they have been working to a detailed schedule adapted to the situation, to remain a stable, meaningful anchor. The schedule includes daily Zoom group activities, remote learning support for those who need it, and individual WhatsApp, video, and Zoom calls—out of a deep understanding that at this time it is important for every child to feel seen, heard, and to see staff faces. In small, consistent steps they are getting through this difficult period.

Alongside routine educational work, guidance staff received dedicated training on the emotional processes youth experience under stress and anxiety, and practical tools for calming, appropriate dialogue, and strengthening respite activities. The organization also recognizes that staff are part of the same complex reality, so daily team Zoom meetings are held for sharing, support, and morale. Community resilience is built through mutuality—care for both youth and staff—and a deep commitment to remain a stable, safe home even in times of uncertainty. They are asking for donations and list a contact number for volunteering.
