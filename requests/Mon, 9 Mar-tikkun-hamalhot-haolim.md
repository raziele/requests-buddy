## The Ask

Tikkun and Hamalhot HaOlim are seeking donations to provide stability and support for children and youth during the "Roaring Lion" operation. They aim to strengthen community resilience, reduce stress and anxiety, create safe spaces, and support local communities through educational programs in municipal shelters, educational centers for evacuated families, programs for teens near Gaza, and community activities tailored to local needs.

**Funding Requested:** $25,000

## Context & Background

On February 28, 2026, Operation "Roaring Lion" began, involving hundreds of missiles and drones launched from Iran toward Israeli communities. Israelis are facing ongoing attacks, compelling them to move into bomb shelters and disrupting daily life. Tikkun and the Hamalhot HaOlim Youth Movement are mobilizing to provide educational and emotional support to strengthen communities and the civilian front.

They are seeking donations to fund volunteer housing, transportation, insurance, staff coordination, training, equipment, food, and more. The estimated cost for the coming week is $25,000.

The initiative focuses on:

*   **Educational programs for children and teens in municipal shelters.**
*   **Educational centers for children from evacuated families.**
*   **Programs for teens in the Eshkol region near Gaza.**
*   **Community activities tailored to local needs and municipalities.**

Their goals are to strengthen community resilience, reduce stress and anxiety, create safe spaces for processing and relief, and support local communities.
