## The Ask

Barak Loozon, Director of the Israel Office of a federation involved in Healthy Mind (Toronto and other federations), is sharing information about Healthy Mind’s national telehealth platform for psychiatric care in Israel. Healthy Mind has treated over 10,000 patients nationwide, partners with the Ministry of Health, Maccabi Health, Clalit Health, and Resilience Centers, and is currently fundraising for two one-time special projects (technology infrastructure and Clalit integration). ## Context & Background

Healthy Mind operates a national telehealth platform that provides psychiatric care across Israel and has been credited with transforming aspects of the Israeli healthcare system. The platform has treated more than 10,000 patients nationwide and works with major health providers including the Ministry of Health, Maccabi Health, Clalit Health, and Resilience Centers. Wait times for psychiatric services have been reduced from many months to a few weeks for patients using the platform.

Barak Loozon was approached by the Healthy Mind initiative (in which his federation in Toronto and several other federations are involved) to share information about their work with interested federations. He notes he does not usually distribute such material broadly but felt obliged to do so given the impact of the work on access to psychiatric care. His federation has provided Healthy Mind with two grants, which he describes as unusual for them.

Healthy Mind is currently fundraising for two special one-time projects: technology infrastructure and Clalit integration. Additional materials are linked via a Canva design (page 3 referenced for the live link). The email is informational and introductory; there is no specific funding amount requested in the message.
