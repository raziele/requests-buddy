A federation professional (Barak Loozon, Israel Office) is sharing information about Healthy Mind, a national telehealth platform that provides psychiatric care and is transforming the Israeli healthcare system. Healthy Mind has treated over 10,000 patients nationwide and partners with the Ministry of Health, Maccabi Health, Clalit Health, and Resilience Centers. ## Context & Background

Healthy Mind is a national telehealth platform in Israel that provides psychiatric care and has partnered with major health providers including the Ministry of Health, Maccabi Health, Clalit Health, and Resilience Centers. The organization has treated more than 10,000 patients nationwide and focuses on improving access to psychiatric services by reducing wait times from many months to a few weeks.

The email is sent by Barak Loozon (Director, Israel Office) on behalf of the Jewish Federation. He was approached by Healthy Mind, which is involved with the Toronto federation and several other federations, to help share information about their work with interested federations. He notes that he typically does not broadcast such information widely but felt obliged to share given the importance of their work in making psychiatric care accessible.

Healthy Mind is currently fundraising for two special one-time projects: technology infrastructure and Clalit integration. Additional materials are linked in the email (Canva design link). The sender offers to explain why his federation provided Healthy Mind with two grants (which they usually do not do) and to facilitate direct connections for those interested.
