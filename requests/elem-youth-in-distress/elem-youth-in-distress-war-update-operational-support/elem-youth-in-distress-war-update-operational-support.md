## Context & Background

ELEM (עלם – Youth in Distress) is updating its funder on operations since the launch of Operation "Roaring Lion." Circumstances continue to shift rapidly; the organization emphasizes that its commitment to the teens and young people it supports remains unwavering in both routine and emergency periods.

Across all programs, teams maintain continuous contact with teens and provide support tailored to emerging needs on the ground. In line with national safety guidelines, most support is currently delivered remotely. Pre-existing trust and relationships have kept these connections meaningful, responsive, and effective.

To ensure an accessible and immediate response for those in distress following recent events, ELEM has expanded several services: Digital Outreach Support now runs on weekdays until midnight with reinforced teams that have received specialized training for this period; 24/7 shelters for young people at the highest end of the risk spectrum in Jerusalem, Haifa, and Tel Aviv have been strengthened in terms of professional staffing so that every shift is fully staffed and able to provide immediate, trauma-informed care. The writer closes by stating that the recipient's support will enable ELEM to respond swiftly, expand services as needed, and maintain operational continuity even under highly challenging conditions.
