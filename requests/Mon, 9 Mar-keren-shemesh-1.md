## The Ask

Keren Shemesh, in coordination with the municipality and local organizations, requires rapid and flexible funding to provide immediate assistance to families affected by a direct strike on a shelter in Beit Shemesh. Priorities include emergency relief for families, strengthening the municipal rescue unit, and upgrading shelters.

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

## Context & Background

A direct strike on a shelter in Beit Shemesh has resulted in casualties and severe distress for many families. Keren Shemesh is actively coordinating with local authorities and organizations to provide immediate on-the-ground assistance. The request emphasizes the need for rapid and flexible funding to address three urgent priorities: emergency relief for families, strengthening the municipal rescue unit with advanced equipment and training, and upgrading shelters and safe rooms. The foundation is prepared to act immediately, and every contribution is crucial for life-saving action and strengthening the city's resilience.
