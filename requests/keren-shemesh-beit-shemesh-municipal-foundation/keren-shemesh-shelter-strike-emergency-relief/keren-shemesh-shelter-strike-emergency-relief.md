## Context & Background

On March 8, 2026, a direct strike on a shelter in Beit Shemesh claimed lives and left many families in severe distress. Keren Shemesh (kerenbshemesh.org.il), the municipal foundation, is working in coordination with the municipality, the municipal welfare department, and local chesed organizations to provide immediate assistance on the ground. Effective response requires rapid and flexible funding.

The foundation has identified three urgent priorities. First, emergency relief for families and shelter activities: basic supplies, food, clothing, emotional support and guidance, and activities for children and families staying in shelters. Second, strengthening the municipal rescue unit, which has over 200 volunteers, with advanced rescue equipment, protective gear, lighting, communications, and training. Third, upgrading and maintaining shelters and safe rooms—renovations, equipment for extended stays, and long-term protective infrastructure.

The foundation is prepared to act immediately subject to available resources. Every contribution enables life-saving action and strengthens the resilience of the community. A donation channel is available via JGive (link provided in the email).
