## The Ask

Mechinat Shuvu Achim, a nationwide humanitarian mechina operating in memory of the three abductees, works with local welfare departments from Kiryat Shmona to Sderot to support vulnerable populations. During Operation Roar of the Lion (Shavat HaAri), the organization has received a surge of requests from the field. They are seeking monetary or in-kind donations to address urgent needs: computers for children in shelters for remote learning, emergency kits for reservists who have been in the field for over a week, and hygiene products, medicine, and basic food for the elderly. The association holds all required documentation for tax purposes and is asking the recipient to consider a partnership or contribution.

## Context & Background

Mechinat Shuvu Achim (Shuvu Achim Mechina) is a pre-military academy and humanitarian project operating nationwide in Israel in memory of the three abductees. It collaborates with welfare departments across the country from Kiryat Shmona in the north to Sderot in the south to assist populations in distress.

During the past week and a half of Operation Roar of the Lion (Shavat HaAri), the organization has been receiving many requests from the field. The needs fall into three main categories: (1) children staying in shelters who are required to learn remotely but whose families cannot afford computers; (2) reservists who were called up at the start of the operation and have been in the field for over a week and need emergency kits; and (3) elderly people who need hygiene products, medicine, and basic food.

The request is directed at exploring the possibility of a monetary donation or in-kind product donation for these populations given the urgency of the situation. The email was forwarded by Dina Berman (Chicago Federation/JUF) to the recipient. The organization states it holds all documents required for tax purposes and expresses gratitude in advance for any consideration and support.
