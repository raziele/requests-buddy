## The Ask

Adi Nir from Yeladim shares an update on the challenging situations faced by children and young adults in Israel due to the ongoing security situation. The organization provides essential support including food, basic supplies, and emotional assistance to vulnerable individuals and families struggling with financial strain, loneliness, and the inability to access basic necessities.

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

**Non-Financial Ask:** Provide steady, reliable support to children and young people.

## Context & Background

In the midst of tense days and ongoing security concerns, Adi Nir from Yeladim provides an update on the difficult realities faced by the children and young adults they support in Israel. Many are experiencing increased instability, financial strain, and loneliness.

Specific cases highlight the severity of the situation: a single mother with three young girls struggles to reach a shelter due to the children's age and the lack of an elevator, forcing them to relocate to the shelter. She also faces significant financial hardship and isolation. Another young adult, a business owner serving in the reserves, experiences a sharp drop in income each time he is called up, jeopardizing his ability to cover expenses. A young woman with a chronic illness finds her condition exacerbated by the security situation, leading her to reduce her food intake. Another young woman is forced to choose between buying medication or food due to her mother's inability to work and the overall household responsibility falling on her. Lastly, a 16-year-old under Yeladim's guardianship, whose mother is ill and institutionalized, faces an empty refrigerator and relies on the organization for basic groceries.

These stories illustrate the broader reality for children and young people growing up without a strong family support system. Yeladim remains in close contact with these individuals, assessing their needs and providing urgent assistance. The organization emphasizes the critical importance of consistent and dependable support during such challenging times.
