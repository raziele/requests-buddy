## The Ask

The Haifa Municipality is requesting donations of 50 laptops and 50 desktops, along with screens, to maintain its operational continuity and efficiency during the ongoing emergency situation. These resources are crucial for the effective functioning of municipal services for the residents of Haifa.

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

**Funding Breakdown:**
- Laptops (50 units): None
- Desktops (50 units): None
- Screens: None

## Context & Background

In light of the current war and the accompanying challenges, the Haifa Municipality is seeking assistance to maintain its operational continuity and efficiency. To this end, they are requesting donations of computer equipment. The specific needs include 50 laptops with the following specifications: Intel Core i7 processor, 512GB SSD storage, 16GB RAM, and Windows 11 Pro operating system. They also require 50 desktop computers and monitors, with specifications to be provided based on available stock and operational needs. This equipment is essential for the effective functioning of municipal services for the residents of Haifa during this critical period.
