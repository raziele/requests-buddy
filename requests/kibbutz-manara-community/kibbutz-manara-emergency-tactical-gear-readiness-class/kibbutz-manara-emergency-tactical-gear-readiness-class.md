## The Ask

Kibbutz Manara is requesting emergency support to equip its community standby/readiness class (30 people) with tactical gear and essentials. Total requested cost is 70,000 ₪. Needs include tactical uniforms, LOWA shoes (flagged as most urgent—members are currently in torn shoes), radio holsters, softshell jackets (AZTEC or OUTDOOR), and Pentel markers. The request follows prior correspondence and asks whether the recipient can assist.

**Funding Requested:** 70,000 ₪

## Context & Background

Kibbutz Manara’s resource development team is submitting a formal request for emergency assistance to outfit its standby/readiness class (כיתת הכוננות). The request is framed as a follow-up to existing correspondence, indicating an ongoing dialogue about community security and readiness.

The needs are specified for 26 of the 30 class members and include: tactical uniforms, LOWA shoes (explicitly marked as the most urgent item, with people currently wearing torn shoes), holsters for communication devices, softshell jackets (AZTEC or OUTDOOR), and 40 Pentel markers in assorted colors. The total cost is stated as 70,000 ₪, but no per-item budget breakdown is provided.

The request is written in Hebrew from the organization’s resource development manager (Iris Hen), who asks directly whether there is a way to assist. The kibbutz is in the Upper Galilee; community readiness and equipment for such units are a recognized priority in the region. Support is being sought to close the equipment gap for the standby class.
