## The Ask

The Association for Israel's Soldiers is raising funds for Operation "Roaring Lion" and its Emergency Relief Fund to support IDF soldiers and their families. Donations will cover essential personal necessities, life-saving medical equipment, and financial aid for wounded soldiers, evacuated families, and bereaved families. 100% of donations directly benefit soldiers with no overhead costs.

**Funding Requested:** Not specified

**Funding Breakdown:**
- Male hygiene kit: 200 NIS
- Female hygiene kit: 150 NIS
- Financial aid for evacuated families: 2000 NIS per family
- Financial aid for moderately or seriously injured soldiers' families: 5,000 NIS per family
- Financial aid for bereaved families: 2,000 NIS per family
- Fully equipped military ambulance: 650,000 NIS

## Context & Background

"The Association for Israel's Soldiers" is a non-profit organization authorized by the IDF to raise funds for the welfare and well-being of IDF soldiers. In light of the "Swords of Iron War" and "Operation Roaring Lion," the association is actively supporting soldiers through its Emergency Relief Fund. This includes providing essential personal necessities, life-saving medical equipment (such as combat medical gear and military ambulances), and financial assistance to families who have been evacuated, wounded, or are bereaved. The operation involved significant military actions, including a large flyover and numerous strikes, with casualties and injuries among soldiers. The association emphasizes that 100% of donations go directly to soldiers.

## The Association for Israel's Soldiers

### Our Mission
The IDF, the State of Israel's military, was founded to defend the country, shield its residents, protect its independence, and foil attempts by its enemies to disrupt routine life. As the foundation of Israel's Ministry of Defense, the IDF and its strategies and tactics are directed by Israel's democratic civil government. IDF soldiers are obligated to devote every effort to protecting the State of Israel at all costs, even if it means putting their lives on the line. Military personnel are sworn to comply with the IDF's commands and values. As such, soldiers are required to abide by Israeli law, uphold human dignity, and respect Israel's principles as a Jewish and democratic state.

The Association for Israel's Soldiers nonprofit is the sole organization that the IDF has authorized to receive and distribute donations for the welfare and well-being of IDF soldiers. This means that 100% of all donations to the nonprofit go directly to benefit the servicepeople. The Association for Israel's Soldiers is proud to support, empower, and care for Israel's soldiers before, during, and after their military service. Numerous programs established and conducted by the association are made possible by the generous donations of supporters, companies, and organizations in Israel and worldwide.
*100% of donations go directly to the soldiers!*

### The IDF Donation Policy
During times of crisis, Israel and its supporters worldwide strengthen their resolve to support and embrace the people of Israel, the IDF and its dedicated soldiers. The IDF is the sole organization responsible for procuring personal protective equipment for soldiers and gear required for military operations. Army policies allow the IDF to receive donations from an emergency fund that can deliver immediate solutions to the IDF's needs and benefit its soldiers.
