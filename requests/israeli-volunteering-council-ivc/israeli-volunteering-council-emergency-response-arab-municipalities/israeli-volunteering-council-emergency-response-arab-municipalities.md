## The Ask

The Israeli Volunteering Council is requesting $179,000 to activate emergency response in 10 Arab municipalities where they already run Volunteer Leader programs. The initiative includes emergency leadership training for Volunteer Leaders; distribution of 100 group shelter kits and 2,000 individual creative kits for children (including homebound/disabled); and knitting kits for older adults in shelters to make octopus dolls for emotionally struggling children. Arab localities have a critical shortage of private shelters (46% of Arab residents without standard protective spaces vs 26% national; only 37 public shelters in Arab municipalities, 8 unfit).

**Funding Requested:** $179,000

**Funding Breakdown:**
- Leadership training workshops (online, 10 municipalities): $25,000
- Group shelter kits (board games, art supplies, Arabic guides) — 100 kits: $50,000
- Individual creative kits (homebound/disabled children) — 2,000 kits: $80,000
- Octopus knitting kits — 300: $9,000
- Logistics and volunteer coordination: $15,000

## Context & Background

Arab municipalities in Israel have significantly lower rates of private bomb shelters (46% of Arab residents without standard protective spaces vs 26% nationally; of 11,775 public shelters, only 37 in Arab municipalities, 8 unfit — Israel Democracy Institute 2024). Families often wait in crowded public spaces (parking lots, schools) with no activities or plan. Arab society was already under stress from internal violence and crime. IVC already operates Volunteer Leader programs in 10 Arab municipalities; this proposal equips those leaders with emergency training and kits. Pillar I: emergency leadership training (resilience, protocols, mapping vulnerable residents). Pillar II: 100 group shelter kits, 2,000 individual creative kits (Arabic). Pillar III: knitting kits for older adults in shelters to make octopus dolls for children. Expected impact: 2,100 children directly; 5,000–7,000 in rotating shelter environments; 100 leaders trained; 10 municipalities equipped.
