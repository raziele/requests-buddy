## The Ask

The Niroot Forum requests an urgent grant of 1,010,000 NIS to distribute essential food vouchers to young adults at risk following the Lion's Roar War. Evidence from an October 2025 needs assessment shows severe employment and financial deterioration, material hardship, and lack of family safety net. The grant will provide 400 NIS in food vouchers to 2,500 high-risk young adults (1,000,000 NIS) plus 10,000 NIS for nationwide coordination and distribution.

**Funding Requested:** 1,010,000 NIS

**Funding Breakdown:**
- Direct aid – 400 NIS food vouchers for 2,500 young adults at high risk: 1,000,000 NIS
- Coordination & logistics – nationwide procurement and distribution across member NGOs: 10,000 NIS

## Context & Background

The Niroot Forum is a national coalition of 37 NGOs supporting 20,000 young adults (18–30) without family support. Following the Lion's Roar War they request urgent assistance. Evidence from October 2025 needs assessment (post "like a lion" war): 78% not working regularly; 70% significant deterioration in financial situation; 21% accumulated new burdensome debts; 36% had to financially support their parents despite their own lack of resources. Acute need for basic survival: food, medicine, utilities. Without a family safety net, young people cannot bridge the gap created by war. The requested grant provides dignity of basic nutrition and stability to navigate ongoing war trauma.
