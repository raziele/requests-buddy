Dear Barak,



I hope you and your family are safe.

I would like to briefly update you on ELEM’s activities since the launch of Operation “Roaring Lion.” Even as circumstances continue to shift rapidly, our commitment to the teens and young people we support remains unwavering: we are there for them, both in routine times and in emergencies.



Across all of our programs, our teams are maintaining continuous contact with the teens and providing support tailored to the needs emerging on the ground. In accordance with national safety guidelines, most of this support is currently delivered remotely. Thanks to the trust and relationships built in advance, these connections remain meaningful, responsive, and effective.



At the same time, we have expanded several services to ensure an accessible and immediate response for those experiencing distress following recent events:

•  Our Digital Outreach Support now operates throughout the weekdays until midnight, with reinforced teams who have received specialized training for this period.

•  In addition, our 24/7 shelters for young people at the highest end of the risk spectrum in Jerusalem, Haifa, and Tel Aviv have been strengthened in terms of professional staffing, ensuring that every shift is fully staffed and able to provide immediate, trauma-informed care.

Your support will enable us to respond swiftly, expand our services as needed, and maintain operational continuity even under highly challenging conditions.



May we soon see quieter and safer days.



Very best,
Dana
