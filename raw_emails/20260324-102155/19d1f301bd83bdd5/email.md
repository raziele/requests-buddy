Dear Rebecca and Barak,
I hope this message finds you and your family well, as much as possible during these very difficult days.

Given the continued escalation, the growing humanitarian needs, and our assessment that this emergency is unlikely to end in the near future, I wanted to reach out to you and the
Jewish Federation Bay Area with an urgent request for support for Pitchon Lev’s emergency humanitarian response in Israel.

Since the first day of the war, Pitchon Lev has been operating continuously in the field. In coordination with municipal welfare departments across the country, we have already distributed a large number of food parcels and provided essential humanitarian aid to vulnerable families in numerous municipalities. At the same time, the needs remain immense, and we continue to receive many urgent requests from local welfare departments for additional assistance.

In parallel, we have deployed a Mobile Rights Utilization Hub in Jerusalem to support evacuated families from Beit Shemesh. Following the recent missile strikes affecting Arad and Dimona, we are now deploying two additional Mobile Rights Utilization Hubs to assist evacuated families from Dimona and Arad in evacuation centers in the Dead Sea area.
These hubs provide vital hands-on assistance to families struggling to access benefits, grants, compensation, and other rights at a time of deep uncertainty and displacement. Alongside our food and emergency aid response, this work has become an essential humanitarian lifeline for vulnerable households.

In light of these expanding needs, we would be deeply grateful if the Jewish Federation of the Bay Area would consider supporting these critical efforts. In addition, given your leadership in working with philanthropists, donor-advised funds, and foundations, we would be especially appreciative if you might also consider raising this appeal with relevant DAF holders and foundations, local or national, who may be interested in supporting urgent humanitarian and welfare efforts in Israel.

Attached is a brief proposal outlining the current emergency programs and budgets in more detail. I would be very glad to provide any further information, answer any questions, or arrange a call at your convenience.

Thank you very much for your consideration, and for the Federation’s continued care and solidarity with Israel during this difficult time.

With warm regards and prayers for safer and better days,

Peleg
