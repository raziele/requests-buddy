These are men and women who risked everything to defend the country—and now need our help to reclaim their livelihoods.



ReStart:HOPE is a community-driven business recovery program supporting over 200 Jerusalem reservist entrepreneurs built on a “reservists-for-reservists” model.



Through this program, participants receive:



  *   Community and peer support via networking events, conferences, and a shared workspace
  *   Tailored workshops, courses and webinars on financial resilience, AI tools, digital marketing and more.
  *   Direct business assistance including mentoring, loan guidance, and automation tools



With your support, we can offer a lifeline—practical, emotional, and economic—to reservist business owners returning from the front lines.
