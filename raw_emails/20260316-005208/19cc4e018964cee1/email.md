Dear Supporters and Federation Leaders,
During these challenging days, we feel more strongly than ever what it truly means to stand together. Knowing that we are part of a broad and deeply committed family gives us strength, resilience, and hope.

We extend our heartfelt gratitude for the support, trust, and dedication you have shared with us. For all of us at the Ethiopian National Project (ENP), your solidarity is a true source of strength. It empowers us to provide a steady, professional, and compassionate presence for those who need it most.

Israel’s schoolchildren have endured years of disruption, uncertainty, and ongoing challenges. Their parents strive to model resilience and stability, even when the burden is immense. For the children participating in ENP’s SPACE program, our educational teams serve as a vital anchor of stability and empowerment- a trusted and professional presence helping Ethiopian-Israeli families navigate a complex reality with hope and confidence.

With thanks to our shared work, the next generation of Ethiopian-Israelis is increasingly integrated into all sectors of Israeli society, playing an active and meaningful role in safeguarding the country’s security, contributing to its defense, its growth and development. We came to Israel with a deep sense of mission and Zionism- to contribute and to help protect and shape the future of the State. We have made significant advances and will continue to do so, grounded in a strong sense of identity and pride, even in times of pain and challenge.

In the months ahead, we must continue to advance our work and strengthen our community while contributing to Israel’s resilience. Designated support of Federations and foundations enables us to broaden our impact and further empower the Ethiopian-Israeli community. We carry this responsibility with deep commitment, and will be proud and honored to share the progress we make in this critical endeavor.

Thank you for standing with us so that we can overcome every challenge together.

With appreciation and respect,
!!עם ישראל חי
Roni Dessie Akale, Director-General, ENP
