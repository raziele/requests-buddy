*Ask Me Anything* is a dialogue program that will bring together Israeli
and American teens for an open and honest conversation about the current
war. Ahead of a Zoom dialogue session, North American participants can
submit questions to their Israeli peers, who respond by sharing personal
experiences and reflections from daily life during wartime.

Questions might include: *Where were you when the war with Iran began? What
was the scariest moment for you? How often do you check the news each day?
What do you do while staying in a shelter?* The conversation will be
reciprocal, giving Israeli teens the opportunity to ask about rising
antisemitism and Jewish life in North America.

The goal of the program is to deepen mutual understanding, strengthen
personal connections, and foster a shared sense of partnership and
solidarity among young people on both sides of the ocean.
