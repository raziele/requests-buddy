I wanted to share with you a brief summary of ZAKA's current rescue needs, whether the Federation will have funds for emergency needs, or for any DAF that may be interested.

Important to note that this equipment is designated for ZAKA’s HFC Rescue Unit, which operates in cooperation with and is trained by the Home Front Command.
