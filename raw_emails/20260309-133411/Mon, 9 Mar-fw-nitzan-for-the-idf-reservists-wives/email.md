I hope I find you well and that you and your loved ones are protected, as much as possible, in these turbulent days.

Throughout the longest war in Israel's history, the wives of the reserve forces were one of the solid pillars on which the entire Israeli society rests. Those dear women, who suddenly became almost single mothers, while allowing their partners to focus on defending the State of Israel and the people of Israel, carried – and still carry – an enormous burden. We must not abandon them alone.

The Nitzan Association wishes to provide them with a process to strengthen their resilience and parental authority. I am attaching for your consideration a request for support for a project for the benefit of these mothers. Please help us provide them with a broad, comprehensive and supportive envelope.

I thank you in advance for your attention and consideration.

Best regards,
