Dear Barak,

Thank you for taking the time to meet with me on Zoom, especially during these challenging times.

I thanked you for your immense partnership in the horse rehabilitation field and explained how your support has enabled us to share the powerful benefits of therapeutic riding, giving participants a sense of confidence, and emotional healing that is truly transformative.

Your partnership means a great deal to TIKVOT. Through your support over the past two and a half years, we have been able to reach and assist so many individuals whose lives have been deeply impacted.

This has been an incredibly challenging time for all of us.  Over the past month, we canceled three ski groups of over 180 participants. Our 18th year of skiing was long waited for and the disappointment was huge. We also canceled a  triathlon that was meant to take place in April. Many of our TIKVOT participants have remained homebound, which is a reality that is both limiting and deeply frustrating for individuals who depend on sports as part of their healing.

Towards the summer, we are focusing on two water bound projects.
One local (immediate impact) + one abroad (deeper rehabilitation journey)

For over 15 years, our waterski program on the Kinneret has been one of the most powerful rehabilitation tools we offer.

Waterskiing has an extraordinary therapeutic impact. With adaptive equipment and professional instructors, even those in wheelchairs, those with prosthetic limbs, and those carrying the invisible weight of trauma are able to rise beyond their limitations.

Alongside this, we are expanding another deeply meaningful initiative, a SUP and paddle program abroad.

This program offers participants a unique space for healing through nature, movement, and shared experience, allowing them to reconnect with themselves, rebuild confidence, and find moments of calm and strength away from daily pressures.

We also see our waterski program in Israel as an opportunity to support communities in the north who have sacrificed so much. By bringing our participants to stay in local guesthouses and tzimmers, we can strengthen the local economy while creating a meaningful and restorative experience for our group.

As we really appreciate your partnership, we would be happy if you or  any members would like to participate in these programs.
