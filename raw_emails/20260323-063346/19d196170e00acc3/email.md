​​EXTERNAL EMAIL - USE CAUTION
I hope this message finds you well, as much as possible during these very difficult days.

First and foremost, I would like to express our deep gratitude for JUF’s support and partnership with Pitchon Lev since the beginning of the war. Your support has been deeply meaningful and has helped us respond to urgent humanitarian needs with speed, professionalism, and compassion. It is precisely because of this valued partnership, and our appreciation for all that JUF has already done, that we have refrained from approaching you again until now.

However, given the continued escalation, the growing humanitarian needs, and our assessment that this emergency is unlikely to end in the near future, we are now turning to you with an urgent request for additional support for Pitchon Lev’s emergency response efforts.

Since the first day of the war, Pitchon Lev has been operating continuously in the field. In coordination with municipal welfare departments across the country, we have already distributed thousands of food parcels and provided essential humanitarian aid to vulnerable families in numerous municipalities.
While much of the country has been disrupted, our teams and logistics network have continued working to ensure that urgent assistance reaches those who need it most.

In parallel, we deployed a Mobile Rights Utilization Hub in Jerusalem to work with evacuated families from Beit Shemesh over the coming weeks. Following the recent missile strikes impacting Arad and Dimona, we are now deploying two additional Mobile Rights Utilization Hubs to support evacuated families from Dimona and Arad in evacuation centers in the Dead Sea area.

These hubs provide vital hands-on assistance to families struggling to access benefits, grants, compensation, and other rights at a time of deep uncertainty and displacement. Alongside our food and emergency aid response, this work has become an essential humanitarian lifeline for vulnerable households.

In light of the expanding needs, we would be deeply grateful if JUF would consider providing additional support for these critical efforts. Based on our longstanding partnership, and the reports we have submitted demonstrating how JUF funds have been utilized by Pitchon Lev, we hope you may be able to give this request favorable consideration.

As I know that JUF has shown meaningful commitment to supporting the Druze community and villages, I am also attaching a formal request letter that Pitchon Lev received from the welfare department of the local municipality of Hurfeish.
We would be very grateful if you could also consider this request and the opportunity to support the village of Hurfeish through Pitchon Lev’s humanitarian response.

Attached is a brief proposal outlining the current emergency programs and budgets in more detail.
I would be very glad to provide any further information, answer questions, or arrange a call at your convenience.

Thank you again for your partnership, your trust, and your continued solidarity during this difficult time.

With warm regards and prayers for safer and better days,

Peleg
