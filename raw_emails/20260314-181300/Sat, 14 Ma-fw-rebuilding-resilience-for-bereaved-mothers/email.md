From: Liat Sade Sternberg <liats@journey4hope.com>




Dear Barak,

I hope this message finds you well.

I’m reaching out to share with you about our initiative that is transforming the lives of families navigating profound loss, Journey4Hope (J4H).

Since October 7, thousands of families in Israel have been impacted by unimaginable tragedy. J4H provides psychological interventions through a unique seven-day journey of resilience.

Our programs bring together bereaved mothers and offer:

  *   Continuity & connection — building a lasting support network.

  *   Competence & empowerment — helping participants rebuild strength and leadership.

  *   Movement & playfulness — reintroducing joy and vitality.

  *   Resilience impact — 100% of alumni report improved wellbeing and family relationships; 25% return to the employment cycle, 30% begin volunteering, and many launch their own support projects.

Looking ahead, J4H is expanding to support bereaved fathers and to develop pathways from bereavement to leadership. With over 350 participants expected in 2025–2026 and more than 100 families already on the waiting list, every donation makes an extraordinary difference.

The cost to provide this life-changing journey is $5,000 per participant, of which families contribute $2,000 individually. Each gift helps close the gap and directly funds critical resilience journeys for those who need it most.
